Who I Am

I'm a London-based law graduate and SQE1 candidate focused on AI regulation, legal technology and automation. My LLB dissertation examined the EU AI Act's alignment with GDPR — covering biometric surveillance, social scoring, manipulative AI, fundamental rights and cross-border compliance.

I don't just study the law of AI — I build with it. Using Claude, Claude Code, n8n, Zapier and prompt engineering, I'm developing AI-assisted legal workflows: SQE performance tracking systems, document generation pipelines and client-intake automation. That intersection of legal rigour and technical capability is my edge.

My background spans luxury retail, property transactions and combat sports coaching — all environments that demand precision, compliance awareness, communication under pressure and structured delivery.
